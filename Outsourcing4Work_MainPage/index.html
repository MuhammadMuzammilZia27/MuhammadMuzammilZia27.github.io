    Who are we?

    outsourcing4work was founded in Germany almost 30 years ago. Today our team consists of 150+ employees living and working remotely in over 20 countries. Over the past three decades, we have evolved from a software manufacturer to a personnel service provider specialising in Indian remote IT professionals, recruitment process outsourcing, digital marketing, and business process outsourcing.

    We discovered the benefits but also the challenges of using international teams and remote working early on. To ensure smooth cooperation, we therefore actively support our customers with certified German IT coordinators. Our many years of experience have led to the creation of a well-established digital infrastructure and communication structure from which our customers benefit. As a 100% remote company, we have made our philosophy of well-functioning remote work a reality – we have been working fully remote for 10 years now.

    outsourcing4work is one of the oldest and most successful 100% remote companies in Germany.
